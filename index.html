Improved Communication

e-mask automatically generates and displays closed captions as you speak. You can benefit from improved communication in situations such as interacting with someone with a hearing disability or temporary injury or even communicating in a noisy environment. e-mask was developed to perform even in the most demanding settings, such as the medical field, where health professionals wear face masks for long periods of time and operate in fast and unforgiving conditions.

Customizable and Fun

e-mask provides a fun way to express your personality by giving you tons of customization options. Change the font colour, add emoji characters, and create fun backgrounds. Create as many designs as you like and have them compliment your outfit or express your mood.

Great for Kids

e-mask conveniently comes in two sizes, making it great for children as young as six years old. The adjustable straps ensure a comfortable and snug fit every time.
