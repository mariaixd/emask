Overview

e-mask automatically generates and displays closed captions as you speak. It improves communication in situations such as interacting with someone with a hearing disability or temporary injury or even communicating in a noisy environment.

e-mask was developed to perform even in the most demanding settings, such as the medical field, where health professionals wear face masks for long periods of time and operate in fast and unforgiving conditions.

e-mask not only reduces communication issues but also provides a fun way to express your personality with text, emoji, and colour. You can create as many designs as you like and have them compliment your outfit or express your mood.

e-mask conveniently comes in two sizes, making it great for children as young as six years old. The adjustable straps ensure a comfortable and snug fit every time.

e-mask is also reusable and easy to clean and sanitize, making it a highly sustainable option.

Highlights

  • Automatically generates and displays closed captions as you speak.

  • Easy to clean and sanitize.
  • Splash-proof.
